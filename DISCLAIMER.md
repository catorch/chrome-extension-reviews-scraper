## Data Handling

Chrome Web Store reviews are user-generated content and may include personal information, author names, profile photos, opinions, language metadata, timestamps, and other sensitive or identifying details.

If you export review data, you should:

- Collect only what you need.
- Use reasonable request delays and avoid high-volume collection unless you have confirmed that your use is permitted.
- Store exported data securely.
- Minimize retention.
- Remove or anonymize personal data when it is not necessary.
- Avoid publishing raw review text, author names, profile photos, or other user data unless you have a valid legal basis.
- Honor deletion, takedown, access, or correction obligations where applicable.
